Common processing steps (performed by _build_marisa_dict.py):
- Unicode normalization (NFKC)
- Strip zero-width characters (e.g., U+200B, U+2060) and ZWJ/ZWNJ
- Replace typographic apostrophes with ASCII
- Trim residual tag fragments at boundaries
- Casefold
- Deduplicate (preserve first occurrence)
- Manual additions
- Integrations/merges where listed below
